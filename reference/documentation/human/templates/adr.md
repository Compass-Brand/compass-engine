## Section Rationale

- Alternatives: prevents re-litigating previously evaluated options.
- Consequences: makes tradeoffs explicit for future maintainers.
- Context: preserves constraints that shaped the decision.
- Decision: records what was chosen and limits future ambiguity.
- Metadata: enables lifecycle tracking and ownership accountability.
- References: links decision to supporting evidence.
